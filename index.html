About Us

Built by artists,
for artists

Flotion Records is an independent record label with a mission to amplify emerging talent. We believe great music deserves to be heard — no matter where it comes from.

Founded with a passion for authentic sound, we've grown into a collective of over 30 unique artists spanning genres from hip-hop and R&B to electronic and pop. With a combined catalog generating over 10 million streams across Spotify and Apple Music, we're proving that independence and impact go hand in hand.

We handle distribution, playlist pitching, marketing, and brand development — so our artists can focus on what they do best: making music.
